About Us

Amana Capital East Africa Limited exists to deliver institutional-grade investment research, advisory, and fund management services rooted in the Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange and East African capital markets. Its core purpose is to equip Tanzanian investors — both at home and across the global diaspora — with the clarity, discipline, and long-horizon perspective needed to build lasting wealth in the region's frontier markets.

Our Story & Principle

"Amana" translates to trust in Swahili and Arabic. It is not just our name; it is the fundamental operating principle that guides every decision we make. Founded on the belief that East African markets possess deep, undiscovered value, we set out to build an institution capable of uncovering it.

We bring institutional-grade research and disciplined investment management to the Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange, aiming to close the information gap and unlock genuine value for our partners and clients.
