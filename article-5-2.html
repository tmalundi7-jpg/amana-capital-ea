Article 5.2 - Building Your First Investment Portfolio

By Amana Capital East Africa - Investor Education Series

Now it’s time to build. A robust portfolio starts with your goal and time horizon.

Define Your Goal and Time Horizon

Saving for retirement in 20 years? More equity risk.

Saving for a house in 5 years? Lean toward bonds and stable dividend stocks.

Generating monthly income? Focus on high-dividend stocks and bonds.

Sample Allocation Framework

Within equities, spread across at least three sectors. Example moderate portfolio: CRDB 20%, TBL 15%, VODA 10%, TPCC 10%, UTT AMIS Bond Fund 35%, cash 10%.

Starting Small You don’t need millions. TZS 500,000 can buy a few shares and a bond fund. The most important step is to start. Time in the market beats timing the market.

Action Step: Write down your target allocation. Compare to actual holdings. Plan to close the biggest gap over three months.
