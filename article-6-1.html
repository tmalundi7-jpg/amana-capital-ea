ow to Open a CSD Account and Buy Your First DSE Stock

By Amana Capital East Africa – Investor Education Series

All the knowledge in the world is useless if you cannot act on it. This article walks you through the exact steps to open a Central Securities Depository (CSD) account and place your first trade on the Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange.

Open a CSD Account

The CSD account holds your shares electronically. You need one to invest.

Step‑by‑step:

Select a licensed stockbroker from the DSE’s official list (available on the DSE website or through our contact page).

Visit the broker’s office with:

A copy of your TIN (Taxpayer Identification Number).

A passport‑sized photograph.

A copy of your national ID, passport, or driver’s licence.

Proof of residence (utility bill or bank statement).

Fill out the CSD account opening forms.

The broker will submit your application to the CSD. You will receive an account number.

Fund your brokerage account (the broker will provide bank details). Minimum amounts vary but can be as low as TZS 100,000.

Place Your First Trade

Once your account is open and funded:

Contact your broker (by phone, email, or in person) and specify:

Ticker (e.g., CRDB)

Quantity (e.g., 100 shares)

Price limit (e.g., “buy at TZS 2,550 maximum”)

The broker executes the order on the DSE’s ATS.

After execution, shares are credited to your CSD account (T+2 settlement).

You will receive a contract note confirming the trade.

Tips for Beginners

Start small. Buy a few shares of a liquid, well‑known company you have researched.

Use limit orders, not market orders, to control the price you pay.

Hold for the long term. Don’t check prices daily hoping to flip a quick profit; that way lie fees and frustration.

The Daily DSE Wrap Connection

Now that you can act, the Daily Wrap becomes your daily briefing. Read it before making any trading decision. It will tell you what happened yesterday, why, and what the broad market context is. Combined with your fundamental analysis, it keeps you informed and grounded.

Action step: If you haven’t already, call or visit a licensed broker this week and start the CSD account process. There is no substitute for actually taking the first step.
