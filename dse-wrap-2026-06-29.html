Amana Capital East Africa — Daily Market Intelligence

Monday, 29th June 2026 | Closing Analysis & Strategic Outlook

The Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange closed higher on Monday, with the All Share Index reaching 4,040.04. While headline turnover eased from Friday’s record, a surge in deals to 4,704 signalled broad‑based local participation. Foreign selling fell to just 3.65% of turnover — the lowest in over a year — confirming that the foreign exit is over and domestic demand now drives the market. The banking sector led, with NMB executing a 108,000‑share block trade, while bond activity highlighted continued institutional demand for long‑dated government paper.
